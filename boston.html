Boston

January 8 - May 30

I didn't know anyone living in Boston before relocating there. Because I moved during the winter, the city felt like it was asleep - most people went home after work since it got dark around 4pm! As a native midwesterner, I had a hard time adjusting to the more independent and blunt attitudes of east coasters in Boston. However, I had a much better time after I met my partner! He showed me around and definitely made the city feel like home.

Activities

Level 99, a venue of small escape rooms and an accompanying restaurant and bar.
Royale, a concert venue and nightclub located in downtown Boston.
Kimball Farm, an outdoor destination with activities like mini golf, bumper boats, and more.
Newbury Street, the main shopping district in Boston's Back Bay neighborhood.
Prudential Center, a large indoor shopping mall in Boston's Back Bay neighborhood.
Museum of Fine Arts, the 20th largest art museum in the world.
