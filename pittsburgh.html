Pittsburgh

May 30 - August 11

I moved to Pittsburgh for a summer internship. The city has been one of my favorite places that I've lived in so far! Before arriving in Pittsburgh, I had no idea what to expect. Needless to say, my expectations were vastly exceeded by the quality asian food and beautiful sights. My favorite part of each day was driving to work because I got to see the beautiful bridges while crossing the Monongahela River. It's definitely a city full of quirky charm and good food.

Activities

Con Alma, a local jazz club with food, drinks, and live music.
The Monongahela Incline, a trolley carriage that transports passengers over Mount Washington.
The Strip District, a popular area near downtown Pittsburgh with many shops and restaurants.
PNC Park, Pittsburgh's baseball stadium that hosts Pirates games.
Phipps Conservatory, a plant conservatory that partners with Carnegie Mellon University.
Carnegie Museum of Art, an art museum in the Oakland neighborhood of Pittsburgh.
