Ann Arbor

August 28 - Present

I moved back to Ann Arbor in August of 2023 to complete the second year of my master's degree. The best part has been living with my cat, Maple, again. It's been great being back in Michigan!

Activities

Wiard's Orchards and Country Fair, a farm that has apple picking and fairs.
Planet Rock, a rock climbing gym with bouldering, top rope, and lead walls.
State Theater, a movie theater near downtown Ann Arbor.
The University of Michigan Museum of Art, an art museum on the UM campus.
The Natural History Museum, a museum at UM that showcases the natural world.
Downtown Ann Arbor, several streets full of bustling restaurants and shops.
